A Visual
Journey

Through the Eleven Gem of Thailand

Natural Wonder

Phuket

01

Thailand’s largest island, known for its white-sand beaches, turquoise waters, and vibrant nightlife. From the calm shores of Kata and Karon Beach to the bustling Patong Beach, Phuket offers a perfect balance of relaxation and excitement.

Jungle Paradise

Khao Sok National Park

02

One of Thailand’s most spectacular natural parks, filled with ancient rainforests, emerald lakes, and towering limestone cliffs. Visitors can explore Cheow Lan Lake by boat, stay in floating bungalows, and spot exotic wildlife like elephants and gibbons.

City of Contrasts

Bangkok

03

A buzzing metropolis where royal temples meet modern skylines. Explore the Grand Palace, cruise along the Chao Phraya River, and savor the city’s famous street food under glowing city lights.

Mountain Charm

Chiang Mai

04

Surrounded by green hills and forests, Chiang Mai blends nature, tradition, and spirituality. Visit Doi Suthep Temple, walk through ancient city walls, and experience peaceful elephant sanctuaries.

Emerald Escape

Phang Nga Bay

05

A breathtaking bay known for its towering limestone pillars, hidden lagoons, and emerald waters. Best explored by kayak, with highlights like James Bond Island and secret sea caves

Coastal Beauty

Krabi

06

Home to spectacular cliffs, mangroves, and turquoise beaches, Krabi is a paradise for nature lovers. Railay Beach and Ao Nang are perfect for swimming, rock climbing, and watching fiery sunsets.

Ancient Glory

Ayutthaya

07

A UNESCO World Heritage site filled with ruined temples and Buddha statues. Once the capital of Siam, Ayutthaya offers a fascinating glimpse into Thailand’s royal past among peaceful rivers and gardens.

Peaceful Valley

Pai

08

A quiet mountain town in northern Thailand surrounded by lush rice fields, canyons, and waterfalls. Famous for its slow-paced life, hot springs, and breathtaking viewpoints like Pai Canyon.

Underwater Wonderland

Similan Islands

09

An island group in the Andaman Sea famous for clear blue water and colorful coral reefs. A dream spot for divers and snorkelers who want to see turtles, reef sharks, and vibrant marine life.

Timeless Heritage

Sukhothai Historical Park

10

The cradle of Thai civilization, filled with ancient Buddha statues, lotus ponds, and temple ruins. Its peaceful atmosphere and golden light at sunrise make it one of the most spiritual places in Thailand.

Island Elegance

Koh Samui

11

A luxurious island known for palm-fringed beaches, waterfalls, and wellness retreats. Visit the Big Buddha Temple, relax in quiet coves, and enjoy world-class resorts under tropical skies.
